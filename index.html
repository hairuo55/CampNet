Messages

Update (2022/12/21): we have proposed Emo-CampNet, which can provide emotional options for generated speech on the basis of text-based speech editing, so as to make generated speech more expressive.

Update (2022/3/20): we have added comparative experiments of CampNet between EditSpeech (part 3) and Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction (part 4) method.

This page shows the samples in the paper "CampNet: Context-Aware Mask Prediction for End-to-End Text-Based Speech Editing".

Experiments were based on VCTK and LibriTTS dataset. All test samples have not appeared in the training set and validation set.

Arxiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2202.09950

    3: Comparison with EditSpeech (deletion, insertion and replacement)

    Note: In this experiment, we take the speech sample on the demo page (Link) of EditSpeech as the standard, then we use CampNet to synthesize the corresponding parallel speech for comparison. Due to the some difference between CampNet and EditSpeech, for example, different vocoders (CampNet-LpnNet, EditSpeech-HifiGAN), this experiment is only used as a reference for audiometry.

    It is worth noting that we directly use CampNet to perform various operations on the speech on the demo page of EditSpeech without retraining or fine-tuneing the model, that is, the following results of CampNet are generated by one shot mode.

    4: Comparison with Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction (replacement)

    Note: In this experiment, we take the speech sample on the demo page (Link) of Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction as the standard, then we use CampNet to synthesize the corresponding parallel speech for comparison. Due to the some difference between Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction and CampNet (for example, the speech in the editing area of Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction is ready-made and the Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction model is only used to adjust the prosody of the editing speech, while CampNet directly synthesizes the speech in the editing area; different vocoders (CampNet-LpnNet, Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction-HifiGAN)), this experiment is only used as a reference for audiometry.

    It is worth noting that we directly use CampNet to perform various operations on the speech on the demo page of Context-Aware-Prosody-Correction without retraining or fine-tuneing the model, that is, the following results of CampNet are generated by one shot mode.
